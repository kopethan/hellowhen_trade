# Future Money and Business Feature Warning

## Status

This document is for future planning only.

The first beta is money-off.

## First Beta Rule

Do not expose:

- wallet
- payouts
- in-platform money trades
- cash trades
- credits
- deposits
- withdrawals
- Airwallex UI
- Stripe UI
- business payment tools
- voucher purchase tools

First beta should focus on service/goods/other exchanges.

## Future Money Features

Future money features may include:

- money Trades
- wallet balance
- held money
- payout-eligible earnings
- payouts
- platform fees
- payment-provider onboarding
- verified user limits
- admin payout review

These features require:

- legal/payment review
- payment-provider approval
- KYC/KYB
- country support
- limits
- fraud controls
- dispute rules
- refund rules
- admin tools
- ledger/audit trail

## Future Business Features

Future Business features may include:

- brand profiles
- business verification
- business catalogs
- platform-level business offers
- campaign-style Needs
- vouchers
- perks
- coupons
- team/member support
- analytics
- higher limits

## Voucher / Coupon Warning

If Hellowhen allows users to buy, hold, redeem, or trade business vouchers/coupons/perks inside the app, this may create additional requirements.

Possible requirements:

- payment-provider support
- refund policy
- expiry policy
- consumer protection review
- tax review
- voucher/gift-card rules
- business verification
- anti-fraud controls
- support/admin tooling

Do not include voucher/coupon purchasing in first beta.

## Safe Roadmap Order

Recommended order:

```txt
1. Free service/goods beta
2. Trust and verification system
3. Plus/Pro subscription documentation
4. Pro multi-person/multi-item Trades
5. Business verification and brand profiles
6. Business catalog without money
7. Money-provider sandbox
8. Verified money beta with low limits
9. Business vouchers/perks only after review
````

## Product Rule

Core service/goods trading should remain available before money and business tools are introduced.

Do not let future subscription or money features damage first-beta marketplace liquidity.
