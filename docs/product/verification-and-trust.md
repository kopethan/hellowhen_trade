# Verification and Trust Model

## Purpose

Verification is a trust and safety layer for Hellowhen Trade.

It is not only a paid feature.

Some users may use the first beta without identity verification, but verification can be required for higher-risk activity, paid tiers, professional tools, money features, or business accounts.

## Verification Types

### 1. Account Confirmation

Basic account confirmation.

Examples:

- email confirmation
- login/session validation
- password reset support

This can apply to all users.

### 2. Identity Verification

Real-person verification.

Examples:

- legal name
- identity document
- selfie/liveness check
- payment-provider KYC when money exists

This may be required for:

- Plus trust features
- Pro features
- money features
- higher limits
- suspicious/risky users
- users with repeated reports
- manual admin review

### 3. Business Verification

Business or brand verification.

Examples:

- registered business name
- business registration number
- business documents
- brand ownership proof
- KYB provider checks
- manual admin review

This may be required for:

- Business tier
- brand profile
- platform-level business offers
- higher limits
- campaigns
- voucher/perk tools
- future business money flows

## First Beta Verification Rule

For first beta:

- normal Free users do not need identity verification by default
- normal service/goods Trades should be accessible without identity verification
- hidden anti-spam and risk limits still apply
- Hellowhen may request verification or restrict activity when needed

## Future Tier Verification

### Free

- No identity verification required by default
- Verification may be requested for safety/risk reasons

### Plus

- Identity verification required for Plus trust features
- Future money access requires verification and provider approval

### Pro

- Identity verification required
- May receive higher limits after trust/history review
- Multi-party features may require additional safety checks

### Business

- Identity verification required for owner/admin
- Business/brand verification required
- Higher-risk business features require manual/provider approval

## Money Feature Verification

Future money features must require verification.

This may include:

- identity verification
- payment-provider KYC/KYB
- country eligibility
- user risk review
- transaction limits
- payout limits
- admin review
- fraud monitoring

Paying for a subscription must not automatically unlock money.

Money access should only be enabled when:

```txt
subscription/feature access
+ identity/business verification
+ provider eligibility
+ platform risk approval
+ country support
```

are satisfied.

## Safety Restrictions

Hellowhen may limit, pause, or restrict any account for:

* spam
* repeated reports
* suspicious behavior
* unsafe content
* fraud risk
* media violations
* dispute abuse
* payment risk
* policy violations

Restrictions may include:

* reduced posting limits
* proposal limits
* media upload limits
* temporary suspension
* verification requirement
* admin review
* feature removal
* account closure

## Product Copy Principle

Avoid saying:

```txt
Verified because paid
```

Use:

```txt
Verified for trust and safety
```

Avoid implying that paid users are automatically safe.

Use:

```txt
Verified users may access higher-trust features after approval.
```
