# Trade rules

## Visibility

- Feed is public.
- Active public trades are visible in the feed.
- Trade detail is public for active public trades.
- Needs are private to owner.
- Offers are private to owner.
- My Trades is private owner management.
- Wallet and ledger are private to owner/admin.

## Expiration

- Needs can expire.
- Offers can expire.
- Trades can expire.
- Expired trades should leave the public feed.

## Owner close

- Owner can manually close a trade.
- Closed trades leave the public feed.
- Closed trades remain visible in owner's private management history.

## Payment

- Fake credits only in Patch 1.
