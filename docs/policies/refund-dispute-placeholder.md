# Placeholder Refund & Dispute Policy

> Documentation note: this is a product/legal-readiness draft only. It is not legal advice and must be reviewed by a qualified lawyer before public launch.

## Purpose

This starter policy explains trade issue handling for the first beta while real-money wallet/payout/refund flows stay disabled.

## First-launch scope

- No wallet top-ups.
- No payouts.
- No production Stripe/Airwallex money movement.
- No in-app money refunds.
- Support tickets, reports, moderation actions, and audit notes are the operational dispute structure.

## Placeholder process

1. User reports content or opens a support ticket.
2. Admin reviews linked profile/trade/need/offer/proposal context.
3. Admin may hide or restore content, restrict users, close content, escalate to support, or document a decision.
4. Support ticket history and audit logs preserve the review trail.

## Future money-launch prerequisites

- separate wallet terms
- payout rules and fee disclosure
- refund rules
- dispute workflow
- payment-provider review
- transaction ledger reconciliation
- low launch limits
- manual payout review
- privacy and data-retention updates

## Lawyer-review questions

- What refund, chargeback, and dispute obligations apply if money is later enabled?
- What seller/provider payout hold periods are allowed and required?
- What escrow, stored-value, marketplace-payment, or money-transmission rules may apply?
- What user notices are required before withholding, refunding, reversing, or releasing funds?
