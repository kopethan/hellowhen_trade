# Placeholder Terms

> Documentation note: this is a product/legal-readiness draft only. It is not legal advice and must be reviewed by a qualified lawyer before public launch.

## Purpose

This starter Terms page supports the first Hellowhen Trade beta, where the product focuses on Need + Offer exchanges, support, reporting, and moderation.

## Placeholder coverage

- account use and login safety
- Need, Offer, Trade, and Proposal expectations
- public content visibility and moderation
- support/report handling
- first-beta money-off constraint
- future policy change process

## First-launch product assumptions

- Wallet, payouts, provider onboarding, and production money movement remain disabled.
- Users can report content and create support tickets.
- Admins can hide/restore content, restrict users, and keep audit notes.
- Service/goods exchanges are not backed by an in-app payment guarantee.

## Lawyer-review questions

- Which legal entity, country, and governing-law language should be used?
- Which consumer, marketplace, platform, and content-liability disclosures are required in launch countries?
- What age minimum and user eligibility rules are required?
- What limitation-of-liability, termination, and dispute-resolution language is appropriate?
- What exact user acceptance and versioning records should be stored?
