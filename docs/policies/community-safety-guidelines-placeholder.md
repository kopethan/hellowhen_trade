# Placeholder Community & Safety Guidelines

> Documentation note: this is a product/legal-readiness draft only. It is not legal advice and must be reviewed by a qualified lawyer before public launch.

## Purpose

This starter policy gives users clear behavior expectations and gives admins a first-launch moderation baseline.

## Placeholder coverage

- respectful Needs, Offers, Trades, and Proposals
- prohibited scams, spam, harassment, fake profiles, unsafe/illegal content, and privacy violations
- reporting and support escalation
- admin hiding/restoring/restricting actions
- safe local-exchange reminders

## Admin-readiness notes

- Report buttons and support escalation are already first-launch safety surfaces.
- Restricted users should be blocked from create/update/delete/propose/message actions.
- Restricted users’ public content should not appear in discovery/profile routes.
- Admin actions should write audit log entries.
